Indhu Chakra Ragas

Kanakangi is the 1st Melakarta raga in the 72 Melakarta raga system of Carnatic music. It is also known as Kanakāmbari in the Muthuswami Dikshitar school. This raga is characterized by its unique combination of swaras (notes) and is considered a Sampurna Raga, meaning it has all the seven notes (Sa, Ri, Ga, Ma, Pa, Dha, and Ni) in its aroha (ascending) and avaroha (descending) patterns.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G1 M1 P D1 N1 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N1 D1 P M1 G1 R1 S

Ratnangi (pronounced ratnāngi) is a Sampoorna Raga, meaning it has all seven notes (sa, ri, ga, ma, pa, dha, ni) in its ascending and descending patterns (Arohana and Avarohana). It is the 2nd Melakarta Raga in the 72 Melakarta Raga system of Carnatic music.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G1 M1 P D1 N1 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N1 D1 P M1 G1 R1 S

Gaana Murthy Raga is a melakarta raga in Carnatic music, belonging to the 22nd melakarta scale, known as “Natabhairavi”. It is characterized by its unique set of swaras (notes) and arohana-avarohana (ascending and descending patterns)
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G1 M1 P D1 N2 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N2 D1 P M1 G1 R1 S

Vanaspati is a melakarta raga in Carnatic music, classified as the 4th raga in the 1st chakra Indu of the melakarta system. Its mnemonic name is Indu-Bhu, and its mnemonic phrase is sa ra ga ma pa dhi ni.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G1 M1 P D2 N2 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N2 D2 P M1 G1 R1 S

Manavati (Maanavathi) is the 5th Melakarta raga in the Carnatic music system. As a Melakarta raga, it is one of the fundamental parent ragas that helps form the basis for many other compositions and janya (derived) ragas.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G2 M1 P D1 N2 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N2 D1 P M1 G2 R1 S

Tanarupi is the 6th Melakarta raga (parent raga) in the Carnatic music system, part of the 72 Melakarta raga framework.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G3 M1 P D1 N3 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N3 D1 P M1 G3 R1 S

Netra Chakra Ragas

Senavati is the 7th Melakarta raga in the Carnatic music system, making it part of the Indu chakra (the first group of Melakarta ragas). As one of the primary 72 Melakarta ragas, Senavati’s structure and notes offer a distinct sound with both devotion and depth.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G1 M1 P D2 N3 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N3 D2 P M1 G1 R1 S

Hanumatodi (also known as Todi) is a profound raga in Carnatic music, known for its highly expressive, intense, and melancholic character. As the 8th Melakarta (parent) raga in the 72 Melakarta system, Hanumatodi is a staple in Carnatic music with a strong devotional and meditative essence.
Scale (Arohana and Avarohana):
Arohana (ascending scale): S R1 G2 M1 P D1 N2 S
Avarohana (descending scale): S N2 D1 P M1 G2 R1 S
